Read about who we are. We're proud of it.

The story of The Mattress Secret started with a simple idea. Figure out a way to help people across America (and maybe the world someday) get a better night's sleep. But let me start out by introducing myself and at the beginning. My name is Tom. After sleeping my whole life on either a cheap foam mattress or a hand-me-down inner spring model, I was ready for a change. Searching for an alternative, I investigated some options; I was trying to figure out if I could find an affordable way to replace my mattress and actually get a comfortable night's rest.

Looking at the Tempir-Pedic mattresses, I was more than impressed by the quality of the material and the feel of lying down on the viscoelastic bed. Yet, to my dismay, when I tried to find out how much it would cost, I was not only disappointed, but also discouraged. Now let me be completely honest. I was shocked. How could such revolutionary technology be priced in a way that no one could afford to buy it? At over $3000 for one of the less expensive models, I was convinced that there must be a way to buy the same type of mattress for less.

After researching various companies online, I was slowly becoming frustrated at the lack of options. Either the product the company sold seemed of poor or substantially lower quality, or the company didn't sell their products directly to consumers, only to large retail stores. Determined to find a way to get the product I needed for the price I was able to pay, I kept searching. But alas, the only thing I managed to find was confusion and discouragement. Yet ironically, the answer was right under my nose the whole time.

You see, my father works in the furniture business as a Sales Rep. This means that he represents various companies who need a way to sell their wholesale products to their retail clients. Luckily for me, when I finally mentioned my problem to my father. He was able to suggest a possible solution. Buy my mattress from one of the companies he used to represent. At first, I was skeptical that the product from one of these companies would be up to the high level of quality I wanted, but I managed to convince my dad to buy one of the mattresses for himself (through a connection he still had at the factory). Arriving quickly, the mattress looked great. Vacuum-sealed directly from the manufacturer, I immediately opened it and was soon able to try it out.

Again, I want to tell you that I'm being completely frank with you when I say that I couldn't believe how good it felt. I just couldn't believe that this mattress wasn't a Tempir-Pedic and didn't cost anywhere near $3000. And that's when I realized what I had to do. You might say its silly of me, but I felt like I had found an amazing way that I could help people all across America. It felt great to actually have thought of a good idea for once, an idea that would help to alleviate the sleep deprived people of the nation. The rest is history.

The Mattress Secret not only aims to help individuals replace their current mattresses, but hopes to one day convince the families all across the nation to invest in their children and replace their out-dated inner spring mattresses with viscoelastic pressure relieving ones: Mattresses that could help parents, and youngsters alike, maximize the amount of rest they could get during stressful times, when a typical night's sleep can sometimes be only 6 hours, or less. For less than the price of the gas you buy in one month, anyone can now purchase an important investment in their future and in their lives. For less than 1/5 of the price of one of the less expensive Tempir-Pedic mattresses, The Mattresses Secret offers a product that is virtually equivalent. We use the same temperature sensitive viscoelastic pressure relieving memory-cell technology, which until now, was well out of the budget of the average American.

About Primo

"What makes this company successful is that we are a family that shares a vision. Everyday, we work together to realize that vision. When you do that, you are a strong company." Hyman Itzkovitz, Founder

Today, Primo International is a leading Canadian importer of fine quality leather upholstery, fabric upholstery, mattresses, futons, casual dining, youth furniture and more. Headquartered in Montreal, Quebec, the company is still a family run business, now led by President and CEO George ltzkovitz.

Primo's steady growth and success is a result of its strong alliance with its suppliers across Asia and its customers worldwide. As a relationship driven company, Primo treats all of its suppliers and clients alike with honesty, fairness and respect. We remain relentlessly committed to satisfy our customers by offering them the highest standards of production and design while maintaining competitive prices.

Primo owns and operates 500,000 sq. ft. facilities in Montreal, QC, and also utilizes independently owned sleeking facilities in California and Virginia to effectively service all of Canada and the continental United States. Always a participant at furniture market events across North America, Primo occupies several permanent showrooms to allow its clients more interactive and convenient representations of its products.

Primo's staff consists of over 250 dedicated employees worldwide. Primo International continues to grow thanks to the shared vision and hard work of its team. As quality is one of Primo's primary goals, it has achieved and maintains the ISO 9001-2008 certification. This important certificate underlines the commitment Primo has made to quality, domestically and abroad.

Primo International is also a Tier II Validated C-TPAT Member. The Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism (C-TPAT) is an innovative government/private sector partnership program that helps to ease and speed secure importing and exporting of goods, which results into reducing the lead time to customers.
